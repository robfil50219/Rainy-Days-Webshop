About Us

Welcome to Rainy Days, the online shop for adventure clothing located in Trondheim, Norway! We understand that the best adventures can happen regardless of the weather, and that's why we specialize in providing high-quality clothing that can withstand any conditions.

At Rainy Days, we're passionate about exploring the great outdoors, and we believe that everyone should have access to gear that's both functional and stylish. That's why we offer a carefully curated selection of adventure clothing from top brands, including waterproof jackets, breathable pants, wool sweaters, and more.

Whether you're planning a backpacking trip, a camping weekend, or a hiking adventure, we've got you covered. Our collection is designed to keep you comfortable and protected in any environment, so you can focus on the experience rather than the weather.

At Rainy Days, we believe that life is an adventure, and we're here to help you make the most of it. Shop our collection today and discover the perfect gear for your next outdoor excursion!
